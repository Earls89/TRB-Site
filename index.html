About Us

The Thunder Rose Band was formed in 1986. They have been playing shows locally,
mostly around Claiborne and Union County, TN, and Bell County, Ky, for well over 30 years.

What We Do!

The Thunder Rose Band is a local favorite! Whether it is local resturaunt needing
entertainment for an evening, or a private event, you can be sure to have a great time!
